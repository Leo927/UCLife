# Social pillar

Four sub-systems that together make UC Life Sim a *life* sim and not just a survival sim. Read each in isolation; cross-links connect them.

| File | What it covers | Phase |
|---|---|---|
| [ambitions.md](ambitions.md) | Long-arc player goals — what to want | 5.0 |
| [newsfeed.md](newsfeed.md) | Date-keyed events surfaced through diegetic channels | 5.1 |
| [relationships.md](relationships.md) | Talk verb, per-NPC opinion, faction reputation | 5.2 |
| [faction-management.md](faction-management.md) | Player runs a small group (FTL/mercenary layer) | 6 |

## Why this order

Ambitions install **what the player wants**. Without targets, social texture is wallpaper.

Newsfeed installs **the world the wants live in**. Without atmosphere, ambitions feel like quest markers.

Relationships install **the verbs that resolve ambitions involving other people**. Without them, "befriend X" can't be done.

Faction management is the late-game escalation once the player has chosen a side.
